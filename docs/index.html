Spend smarter. Save more.

Vaultie is a subscription tracker that keeps all your recurring payments in one place — so you cancel what you don't use, avoid surprise charges, and keep more money every month.

What Vaultie does

  • 📊 See your total monthly and yearly subscription spend at a glance.
  • 🔔 Get reminders 3, 2 and 1 days before each renewal.
  • 🔒 Your subscription data stays on your device.
  • 🇱🇹 Fully bilingual — Lithuanian and English.

Privacy first

Your subscriptions are stored locally on your device. We only use your email to sign you in. Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
